Procedures

A/T Gear Position Indicator Panel Light Bulb Replacement
1. Remove the shift lever.
2. Remove the A/T gear position indicator panel light socket from the indicator panel.
3. Remove the indicator panel light bulb from the socket, and replace the bulb with a new one.
4. Install the new bulb in the socket, and install the socket in the indicator panel.
5. Install the shift lever.
