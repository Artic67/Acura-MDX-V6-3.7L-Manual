Timing Belt Tensioner: Service and Repair

Timing Belt Adjuster Replacement

1. Remove the timing belt.
2. Remove the battery clamp bolt from the back cover.
3. Remove the auto-tensioner.
4. Remove the bolt (A), then remove the timing belt adjuster (B) and collar (C).





5. Replace the timing belt auto-tensioner and timing belt.
