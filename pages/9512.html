Fuel Pump Control Unit: Service and Repair

Fuel Pump Control Module Replacement

1. Remove the cargo floor lid.
2. On models with active suspension, remove the active damper control unit.




3. Disconnect the fuel pump control module connector (A).
4. Remove the fuel pump control module (B).
5. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal.
