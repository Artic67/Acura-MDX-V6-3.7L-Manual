Variable Induction Control Actuator: Service and Repair

IMT Actuator Replacement

1. Remove the engine cover.




2. Disconnect the IMT actuator 5P connector (A).
3. Remove the bolts (B) and the IMT actuator (C).
4. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal with a new O-ring (D).
