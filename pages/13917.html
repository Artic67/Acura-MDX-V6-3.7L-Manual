Vehicle - TQI and New Model Information: Overview

06-037

November 30, 2006

Applies To:
2007 MDX - ALL

2007 MDX: TQI and New Model Information

(Supersedes 06-037, dated October 6, 2006 to update the information marked by the black bars and asterisks)

This bulletin gives you the information needed for the TQI of the 2007 MDX. It also describes some of the vehicle's new features, equipment, and service information.

NOTE:
To complete the TQI on the MDX with the Navigation System, refer to Service Bulletin 06-038, 2007 MDX: TQI of the DVD Navigation System With Voice Recognition.

The 2007 MDX comes in these models:

^ MDX

^ MDX Tech (Technology Package)

^ MDX Tech with Entertainment Package

^ MDX Sport (Sport Package Added to Technology Package)

^ MDX Sport with Entertainment Package

The Technology Package (Tech models) includes these features:

^ Navigation System and Rearview Camera

^ AcuraLink and Real-Time Traffic

^ ELS Surround Sound with Dolby Pro Logic II

^ DVD Audio

The Sport Package (Sport models) adds these features to the Tech models:

^ Active Damper System

^ Auto-Leveling Headlights

^ Exclusive Alloy Wheels

^ Premium Leather

^ Textured Trim on Center Console and Front Control Panel

The Entertainment Package adds these features to the Tech or Sport models:

^ Rear Entertainment System

^ Power Tailgate

^ Seat Heaters for Second Row Outboard Seats

^ Third Row Floor Mats

^ 115-volt AC Power Outlet

This bulletin includes these TQI procedures:

^ Install the Fuses

^ Install the Shift Lock Release Cover

^ Enter the Audio and Navigation System Anti-Theft Codes

^ Set the Clock and Calendar (Models Without Navigation)

^ Remove the Protective Coverings From the Interior

^ Remove the Protective Coverings From the Exterior

^ Install the Floor Mats

^ Check/Set the Tire Pressures (Including the Spare Tire)

^ Install the Front License Plate Holder

^ Install the Front Strakes

^ Install the Body Plugs

^ Set Up the Rear Entertainment System (If Equipped)

^ Activate the Remote Driver's Seat Memory

^ Check the Moonroof One-Touch Feature

^ Check/Refresh the XM(R) Satellite Radio Dealer Demo Service

^ Do the HandsFreeLink Operational Check

^ Reset the AcuraLink Control Unit

^ Do the Idle Learn Procedure

^ Do the CKP-Pattern Learn Procedure

*^ Check/Clean the Front Grilles*

^ Complete the Personalized Settings Check Sheet (To Be Done by Sales Staff)

This bulletin includes this new model information:

^ HDS (Honda Diagnostic System) Software Version

^ Horsepower

^ SH-AWD (Super-Handling All-Wheel Drive) System

^ Active Damper System (Sport Models Only)

^ VSA (Vehicle Stability Assist)

^ Trailer Stability Assist

^ HVAC - Triple-Zone Climate Control with Humidity Control and Partial Recirculation

^ Electric Tilt/Telescopic Steering Wheel

^ Power Tailgate (Entertainment Package)

^ Second Row Seat Heaters (Entertainment Package)

^ HFL (HandsFreeLink)

^ HFL Phone Book Importation (Models With Navigation)

^ AcuraLink and Real-Time Traffic (Models With Navigation)

^ MID (Multi-Information Display)

^ Type-6 Immobilizer System

^ Retractable Master Key

^ Key and Remote Transmitter Operation

^ Auto-Up/Down and Auto-Reverse Front Windows, and Moonroof Functions

^ Driver Position Memory and Keyless Memory Settings

^ Side Mirrors with Automatic Reverse-Gear Tilt-Down

^ Auto Dimming Instrument Panel

^ One-Touch Starter System

^ Sequential SportShift

^ Shift-Hold Control

^ Maintenance Minder

^ TPMS (Tire Pressure Monitoring System)

*^ Automatic Headlight Leveling System (Sport Models)*

^ DRL (Daytime Running Lights)

^ HID Headlights With Theft Protection

^ LED Lighting

^ SVC (Speed-Sensitive Volume Compensation)

^ Active Front Head Restraints

^ Towing

^ Electric Fuel Fill Door

^ A/C Power Outlet (Entertainment Package)

^ Center Console

^ Front Suspension Hydro Bushing

^ Main Fuse Box Multi-Fuses

*^ Rear Grab Handle*

^ SRS Service

^ Service Connector Location

WARRANTY CLAIM INFORMATION

None. The information in this service bulletin is considered part of the normal TQI.
