Audio System - Radio Loses Station Presets

08-007

September 26, 2008

*Applies To:
2007 MDX - ALL Models Without Navigation (VINS Beginning with 2HNYD282)
2008 MDX - ALL Models Without Navigation (VINs Beginning with 2HNYD282)
2009 MDX - Models Without Navigation
From VIN 2HNYD282.9H500001 thru 2HNYD282.9H504074*

Audio Unit Loses Audio Presets

(Supersedes 08-007, dated March 11, 2008, to update the information marked by the black bars and asterisks)

SYMPTOM

When the different keyless remotes are used (Driver 1 and Driver 2), the audio unit intermittently does not retain the correct audio presets.

PROBABLE CAUSE

An internal problem with the audio unit does not allow it to recognize the keyless remote being used.

CORRECTIVE ACTION

Replace the audio unit with a remanufactured unit.

NOTE:
The inventory of remanufactured audio units has been updated to recognize the keyless remote being used.

PARTS INFORMATION

For information on audio unit ordering, see Service Bulletin 06-001, Audio, Navigation, and RES Unit In-Warranty Exchange, and Audio and DVD Player Out-of- Warranty Repair

WARRANTY CLAIM INFORMATION

In warranty:
The normal warranty applies.

Operation Number: 0101B5

Flat Rate Time: 0.7 hour

Failed Part: P/N 39012-STX-A02

Defect Code: 03214

Symptom Code: 04220

Skill Level: Repair Technician

Out of warranty:
Any repair performed after warranty expiration may be eligible for goodwill consideration by the District Parts and Service Manager or your Zone Office. You must request consideration, and get a decision, before starting work.

DIAGNOSIS

*1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II), then turn the audio unit on, and make sure it operates normally.*

2. Turn off the audio unit.

3. Press and hold the No.4 and No.6 preset buttons, then turn on the audio unit.

4. Make sure DIAGNOSTIC MODE appears on the display, then press the No.2 preset button.

* Is the Main software version 3.0 or higher?

Yes - Disregard this service bulletin, and continue with normal troubleshooting.

No - Go to REPAIR PROCEDURE.*

REPAIR PROCEDURE

Replace the audio unit.
