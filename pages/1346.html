Intermediate Shaft Removal

Intermediate Shaft Removal
1. Drain the transmission fluid.
2. Remove the right driveshaft.
3. Remove the exhaust pipe A.
4. Remove the exhaust pipe bracket (A).





5. Remove the flange bolt (A) and two dowel bolts (B).





6. Remove the intermediate shaft (C) from the differential. Hold the intermediate shaft horizontally until it is clear of the differential to prevent damaging to the differential oil seal.
