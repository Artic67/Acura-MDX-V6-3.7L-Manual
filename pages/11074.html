Suspension - Buzz/Rattle From R/H Front of Vehicle

07-014

March 2, 2007

Applies To:
2007 MDX except Sport - From VIN 2HNYD2...7H500001 thru 2HNYD2...7H515191

Buzz or Rattle From the Right-Front Area of the Suspension

SYMPTOM
During turns, a buzz or rattle comes from the right front area of the suspension.

PROBABLE CAUSE
The right-front damper spring is contacting the inner fenderwell.

CORRECTIVE ACTION
Replace the right-front damper spring.

REQUIRED MATERIALS
Gray Brushable Seam Sealer: 3M P/N 051135-08656
(One can repairs about 20 vehicles.)

PARTS INFORMATION

Right-front Damper Spring: P/N 51401-STX-A02

WARRANTY CLAIM INFORMATION

Operation Number: 414131

Flat Rate Time: 1.1 hours

Failed Part: P/N 51401-STX-A02

Defect Code: 07403

Symptom Code: 04201

Template ID: 07-014A

Skill Level: Repair Technician

Out of warranty:
Any repair performed after warranty expiration may be eligible for goodwill consideration by the District Parts and Service Manager or your Zone Office. You must request consideration, and get a decision, before starting work.

DIAGNOSIS
Check for a wear mark on the inner fenderwell. Turn the steering wheel, and check for spring contact with the inner fenderwell.

REPAIR PROCEDURE

1. Remove the right-front damper assembly:

^ Refer to page 18-21 of the 2007 MDX Service Manual, Volume 2, or

^ Online, enter keyword SUSP and select Front Damper/Spring Removal and Installation from the list.

2. Replace the right-front damper spring:

^ Refer to page 18-28 of the service manual, or

^ Online, enter keyword SUSP and select Front Damper/Spring Disassembly, Inspection, and Reassembly from the list.

3. Repair any wear mark on the inner fenderwell by removing any corrosion and applying seam sealer.

4. Install the damper assembly.
