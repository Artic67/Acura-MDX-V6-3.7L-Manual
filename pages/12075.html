Power Trunk / Liftgate Control Module: Testing and Inspection

Resetting the Power Tailgate Control Unit
If the battery terminal is disconnected or No. 7 (10 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box is removed while the power tailgate is operating, the power tailgate will not be able to open or close automatically until it is reset.

To reset the system, fully close the tailgate manually. Once the battery terminals are reconnected or No. 7 (10 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box is replaced, the power tailgate system will automatically reset. Make sure the power tailgate operates properly.
