Front Fender Fairing

Front Fender Fairing Replacement

NOTE:
- Put on gloves to protect your hands.
- Take care not to scratch the body.

1. Remove the front inner fender as needed.




2. Pull out and remove the front fender fairing (A).
3. Install the fender fairing in the reverse order of removal.
