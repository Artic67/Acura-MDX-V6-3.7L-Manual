Fuel Fill Door Opener Solenoid Test

Fuel Fill Door Opener Solenoid Test

NOTE: Before testing, check the B-CAN System Diagnosis Test Mode A.

1. Open the tailgate, and remove the left side trim panel.




2. Disconnect the 2P connector (A) from the fuel fill door opener solenoid (B).
3. Check solenoid operation by connecting power to the No. 1 terminal and ground to the No. 2 terminal. To prevent damage to the solenoid, apply battery voltage only momentarily. The solenoid should work.
4. If the solenoid does not work as specified, replace it.
