Glove Box Lamp: Service and Repair

Glove Box Light Replacement
1. Remove the glove box stops and damper.




2. Turn the bulb socket (A) 45° counterclockwise to remove it.
3. Remove the bulb (B) from the socket.
4. Install in the reverse order of removal.
