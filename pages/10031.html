PCM Updating and Substitution for Testing

Special Tools Required
^ Honda diagnosis system (HDS) TDSGD2200
^ Honda interface module (HIM) EQS05A35570
^ HDS pocket tester TDSS3557011401
These special tools are available through the American Honda Tool and Equipment Program 1-888-424-6857.

Use this procedure when you have to update the PCM during troubleshooting procedures.
