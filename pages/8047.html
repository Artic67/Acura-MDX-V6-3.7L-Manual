Fluid Type Specifications




Automatic Transmission Fluid Type


Type Use Honda ATF DW-1
(ATF Z1 has been replaced by ATF DW-1, Honda Service News Nov 2010).


Note: You may use DEXRON III automatic transmission fluid as a temporary replacement. However, continued use can effect the shift quality. Have the transmission flushed and refilled with Honda ATF DW-1 as soon as it is convenient.
