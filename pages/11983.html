Rear Bumper Air Spoiler

Rear Bumper Air Spoiler Replacement

NOTE:
- Put on gloves to protect your hands.
- Take care not to scratch the rear bumper.

1. Remove the rear bumper.




2. Remove the bolts (A) and clips (B) then remove the rear bumper air spoiler (D) from the rear bumper (E). Take care not to scratch the rear bumper.
3. Install the spoiler in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
- If the clips are damaged or stress-whitened, replace them with new ones.
- Push the clips into place securely.
