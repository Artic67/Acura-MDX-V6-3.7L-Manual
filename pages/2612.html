RES disc does not eject

RES disc does not eject

NOTE: Discs with labels should not be used in the DVD player unit (built into the audio unit). They may damage the player mechanism.

1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
2. Try ejecting a disc.

Does the disc eject?

YES - Intermittent failure, the DVD player unit (built into the audio unit) is OK at this time.

NO - Replace the audio unit.
