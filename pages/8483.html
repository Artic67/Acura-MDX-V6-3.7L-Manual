Oil Spray Jet: Service and Repair

Oil Jet Replacement

1. Remove the crankshaft from the engine block.
2. Remove the oil jet bolt (A), then remove and discard oil jet (B).





3. Carefully install the new oil jet and tighten the oil jet bolt.
4. Install the crankshaft.
