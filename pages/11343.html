Rear Climate Control Panel Removal/Installation

Rear Climate Control Panel Removal/Installation
SRS components are located in this area. Review the SRS component locations and the precautions and procedures before doing repairs or service.

1. Remove the HVAC trim.




2. Remove the self-tapping screws and the rear climate control panel (A).
3. Install the control panel in the reverse order of removal. After installation, operate the various functions to see whether works properly.
4. Run the self-diagnostic function to confirm that there are no problems in the system.
