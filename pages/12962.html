Cargo Lamp: Service and Repair

Cargo Area Light Test/Replacement
1. Open the tailgate.




2. Carefully pry out the cargo area light (A).
3. Disconnect the 2P connector (B) from the light.
4. Check for continuity between the No. 1 and No. 2 terminals. There should be continuity. If there is no continuity, check the bulb (C). If the bulb is OK, replace the trunk light.
