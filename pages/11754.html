Audio System - Speakers Pop When Using Power Locks

07-062

December 14, 2007

Applies To:
2007 MDX- From VIN 2HNYD282.7H500001 thru 2HNYD282.7H513029

Speakers Pop When Power Door Locks Are Used

SYMPTOM

The speakers pop when the power door locks are used to lock or unlock the doors.

PROBABLE CAUSE

The door lock actuators create an electrical noise that interferes with the audio system.

CORRECTIVE ACTION

Replace the audio unit with a remanufactured unit.

NOTE:
The inventory of remanufactured audio units has been updated to suppress the electrical interference.

PARTS INFORMATION

For information on audio unit ordering, see Service Bulletin 06-001, Audio, Navigation, and RES Unit In-Warranty Exchange, and Audio and DVD Player Out-of Warranty Repair.

WARRANTY CLAIM INFORMATION

In warranty:
The normal warranty applies.

Operation Number: 0101B5

Flat Rate Time: 0.7 hour

Failed Part: P/N 3901 2-STX-A02

Defect Code: 03214

Symptom Code: 03267

Skill Level: Repair Technician

Out of warranty:
Any repair performed after warranty expiration may be eligible for goodwill consideration by the District Parts and Service Manager or your Zone Office. You must request consideration, and get a decision, before starting work.

REPAIR PROCEDURE

Replace the audio unit.
