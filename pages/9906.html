Shift Lever Knob Replacement

Shift Lever Knob Replacement
1. Grasp the shift lever boot ring, and slip it out of the shift lever knob cover.





2. Remove the screws (A) and remove the shift lever knob (B).





3. Apply non-hardening thread lock sealant to the screw threads.
4. Install the new shift lever knob, and secure it with the screws.
5. Slide the shift lever boot ring over the shift lever knob until it snaps on.
