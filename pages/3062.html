Center Pocket

Center Pocket Removal/Installation

1. Remove the audio unit.




2. Remove the bolts (A) and screws (B), then remove the radio brackets (C) from both sides of the audio unit (D).




3. Remove the screws, then remove the center pocket (A).
4. Install the center pocket in the reverse order of removal.
